About Me

Hi there; you seem to have found me. As you might have noticed on the home page, I'm RiskoZoSlovenska, but you can call me Risko. I'm just your average human male, albeit one with an inexplicable attachment to this one sick pair of hunting boots I got from Decathlon. I'm from Slovakia and I used to live in Canada, but nowadays I take up residence in my own head. There's nothing in this world I love more than programming, especially in Lua, but I (very) occasionally dabble in creative writing as well.

Other than that, I'm a music (listening) fanatic (go check out Aviators), a fan of the Ori, R&C and SCP franchises, a FOSS advocate (you should really consider using Linux if you aren't already) and an amateur hobbyist CADer. I tend to be quiet (soooo original, I know), but if you ever catch me ranting, it'll probably be me explaining how some cool computer thing works to my friends. Oh, also, if you're interested, go gawk at my PC build :D

What about this website? What's it for?

Ah, glad you asked! (Or scrolled. Same thing.)

Honestly, hard to say. It's just a place on the world wide web to call my own, and somewhere where I can host things like stories, projects, docs (TBA), games and other stuff. I'm trying to balance being serious with being chill, so please excuse any tonal discrepancies you may come across.

On a more technical side, I'm striving for something simple yet expressive here, with emphasis on simplicity and semantics rather than compatibility. If something doesn't work for you, stop using an old/obscure browser. If it still doesn't work, shoot me an email or a DM, or open an issue. Bonus points if you make it sound like an angry complaint to tech support :P
